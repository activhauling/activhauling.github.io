Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dispatching, support services, and policies.

1. What services do you provide?
We provide professional truck dispatching services including load sourcing, rate negotiation, broker communication, paperwork coordination, invoicing support, and ongoing operational assistance.
2. Which states do you operate in?
We operate across all 48 continental United States.
3. How are payments handled?
Payments are billed monthly for services already rendered. All pricing is disclosed upfront.
4. What types of carriers do you work with?
We work with owner-operators and small to mid-sized fleets operating box trucks, dry vans, reefers, and power-only units.
5. Do you require a long-term contract?
No. There is no binding long-term contract. You may cancel anytime after clearing outstanding dues.
6. How do you charge for dispatch services?
We charge either a percentage of gross revenue or a flat fee, depending on your setup. This is always disclosed upfront.
7. Are there any upfront or hidden fees?
No. We do not charge hidden fees or surprise costs.
8. Do you assist with factoring or payments?
Yes. We assist carriers with factoring setup and payment workflows through trusted partners.
9. Do you guarantee loads or income?
No. Load availability and income depend on market conditions, carrier performance, and lane availability.
10. How do you communicate with carriers?
We communicate via phone calls, email, and messaging platforms to ensure fast and clear coordination.
11. Can I choose which loads to accept?
Yes. The final decision on accepting or declining a load is always made by the carrier.
12. What if I want to stop using your services?
You may stop services at any time after settling any pending invoices.
