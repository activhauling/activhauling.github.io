Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our dispatching, back-office, and support services.

1. What services do you provide?
We provide professional truck dispatching, rate negotiation, broker communication, paperwork coordination, invoicing support, and operational assistance.
2. Which states do you operate in?
We operate across all 48 continental United States.
3. How are payments handled?
Payments are billed monthly for services already rendered.
4. What types of carriers do you work with?
Owner-operators and small to mid-sized fleets operating dry vans, reefers, box trucks, flatbeds, and power-only units.
5. Do you require a long-term contract?
No. You may cancel anytime after settling outstanding dues.
6. Are there any hidden fees?
No. All pricing is disclosed upfront.
7. Can I choose which loads to accept?
Yes. The final decision always belongs to the carrier.
8. Do you guarantee loads or income?
No guarantees are provided due to market conditions.
