Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?
We provide professional truck dispatching services, including load sourcing, rate negotiation, carrier support, invoicing coordination, and ongoing operational assistance.

Which states do you operate in?
All 48 continental U.S. states.

How are payments handled?
Monthly billing for services already rendered.

What types of carriers do you work with?
Owner-operators and small to mid-sized fleets operating box trucks, dry vans, reefers, and power-only units.

Do you require a long-term contract?
No binding long-term contract. You may cancel anytime after clearing dues.

How do you charge for dispatch services?
Percentage of gross revenue or flat fee, disclosed upfront.

Are there any upfront or hidden fees?
No hidden fees.

Do you handle factoring or payment setup?
Yes, we assist with factoring and payment workflows.

Do you guarantee loads or income?
No guarantees due to market conditions.

How do you communicate with carriers?
Phone, email, and messaging platforms.

Can I choose which loads to accept?
Yes, final decision is always yours.

What happens if I want to stop using your services?
Cancel anytime after settling pending invoices.
