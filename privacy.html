Privacy Policy

Last updated: September 2025

ActivTruckin, LLC DBA ActivHauling (“we”, “our”, or “us”) respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Business information (if applicable)
  • Billing information (processed securely via Stripe)

Automatically Collected Information:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent on the website
  • Referring URLs
2. How We Use Your Information
  • Provide and improve our dispatch and back-office services
  • Process payments for agreed-upon services
  • Communicate regarding services, updates, or support
  • Prevent fraud and maintain platform security
  • Comply with legal and regulatory obligations

We do not charge users arbitrarily. All charges apply only to services rendered and agreed upon in advance.

3. Payments & Third-Party Processors

Payments are processed securely through Stripe, Inc.. ActivHauling does not store or process your credit or debit card details. Stripe’s use of your data is governed by their own Privacy Policy.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent personal information. We may share data only with:

  • Payment processors (e.g., Stripe)
  • Trusted service providers assisting website operations
  • Legal authorities when required by law
  • Business successors in case of merger or acquisition
5. Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and physical safeguards to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Data Retention

Personal data is retained only as long as necessary to fulfill service obligations or legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may request access, correction, or deletion of your data. To exercise your rights, contact us using the information below.

8. Cookies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience and analyze traffic. You may disable cookies via your browser settings.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Updates will be posted on this page with a revised date.
